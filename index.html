OUR STORY

It began with a question that would not go away.

RERWA's story began with Sharon, RERWA's Founder, pondering questions shaped by observation, her own realities, and conversations with people from every walk of life. Why do so many people perform for recognition instead of acting from conviction? Why do so many chase success without ever reaching significance? Why do so many carry real potential with no sense of direction?

The pattern was the same everywhere — expanding individuals, but contracting communities; families without structure; organizations obsessed with performative narratives instead of the real impact they were built to create. Out of these questions, a vision was birthed: to raise a generation that thinks and acts from conviction, solving problems beyond personal ambition.

RERWA is a Kinyarwanda word that translates directly to "be raised" — in the sense of holistic parenting. It is the imperative form of the verb kurera, to raise. It invites everyone, at every level, into the continuous process of formation and learning required to live a life of meaning and greater impact.
